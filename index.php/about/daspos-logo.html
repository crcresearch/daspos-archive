Download the DASPOS Logo

The DASPOS logo is the acronym for Data and Software Preservation for Open Science with the P for Preservation highlighted with a green circle. An embellished form of the logo has wires flowing in and out of the Preservation P carrying squares representing data. The data flowing in from one side of the P is mirrored on the other side, suggesting preservation and reproducibility.
